## All Services

### Catalog Management

- **Dynamic Product Listings:** Easily manage and categorize your inventory with our dynamic product listing features.
- **Product Details:** Provide comprehensive product information, including images, descriptions, prices, and stock levels.
- **Search and Filter:** Advanced search and filtering capabilities help users find products quickly.

### Shopping Cart

- **Session-based Carts:** Users can add items to their shopping cart, which persists across their session.
- **Cart Operations:** Support for adding, removing, and updating items in the cart, along with quantity adjustments.
- **Checkout Process:** Seamless integration with checkout processes, including order review and payment.

### Order Management

- **Order Tracking:** Users can place orders and track their status from pending to delivery.
- **Payment Integration:** Supports multiple payment gateways for processing transactions securely.
- **Customer Accounts:** Users can create accounts to manage their orders, view history, and save payment methods.

### Analytics and Reporting

- **Sales Reports:** Generate detailed reports on sales performance, popular products, and customer behavior.
- **Inventory Management:** Track inventory levels, get restock alerts, and manage suppliers directly from the dashboard.
- **Customer Insights:** Gain insights into your customer base, including demographics and purchasing habits.

### Security and Compliance

- **Data Encryption:** All sensitive data, including personal and payment information, is encrypted for security.
- **Compliance:** Designed to meet regulatory standards and compliance requirements for e-commerce operations.
